    BEST ACCEPTABLE ADDRESSES

  1. Warehouse or Staffed Office Space

  2. Must be able to receive mail at location

  3. Should be in service area/territory you own

  4. Once the GBP profile is verified we will remove the address and change from a pin to a service area. Per Google guidelines, companies that service customers at their homes and not at the business location should be set up as service areas. (per Google guidelines our companies service customers in their homes so they should be set up as a service area)

  5. The business location must have clear business signage around the property and/or on the building/door

CAN BE ACCEPTED

  1. Co-working Spaces

  2. These can also be referred to as a "regus office" or "virtual office"

  3. Businesses can't list an office at a co-working space unless that office maintains clear signage, receives customers at the location during business hours, and is staffed during business hours by your business staff.

  4. If they do not have clear signage & are not staffed 8hours a day/5days a week then they are not eligible for a GBP profile

  5. Residential Address

  6. Must be able to receive mail at location

  7. Should be trusted affiliate of business

  8. Must be in service area/territory you own

  9. Once the GMB profile is verified we will remove the address and change from a pin to a service area (per Google guidelines, our companies service customers in their homes so they should be set up as a service area)

  10. Could lead to a reverification request

NOT ACCEPTED (Can result in account level suspension)

  1. Rent a Mailbox (P.O. Box, UPS, etc.)

  2. If you do not operate out of the physical location this would be considered a "virtual office"

  3. These are not eligible for a GMB profile
